More about "Fashion is more than clothing "

I had just finished interviewing my friend for a series on talented black women (coming soon) for National Womens month and we got to talking about fashion. I asked her a question, “Why do designers make abstract clothes that nobody is going to wear on the street? Whats the point?” I figured since she is a fashion design student and has done a fashion show herself that she would know the answer to my question. What she said changed my perspective on fashion.
“Fashion is art” I had never heard fashion explained in this way. I didnt even know fashion could be considered art. But what is art anyway? Is it a drawing, painting, sculpture, architecture? Art is all these things and a little more.
