Fashion is more than clothing

Jan 12, 2022
“Art is the expression or application of human creative skill and imagination” — Oxford Dictionaries

Art doesnt just appear in a museum or on a slab of a canvas. Art is everywhere. Its in the music we sing, the words we write, and the clothes we make.

Its an expression of our creative imagination whether it be simple or abstract. Art isnt defined by a group of thought leaders or Merriam Websters Dictionary. Art is whatever you deem it to be.

Fashion is art. Its a form of creative expression carried out by a designer and put on our blank canvassed bodies.

When we start to look at fashion, and any other creative skill, our perception of it will change and we will have a better appreciation for it.
